Footwear

Wear waterproof shoes with good grip. February can be slushy, with melting snow and unexpected ice patches. Avoid leather solesâ€”they're dangerously slippery.

What to Pack

  • Warm, waterproof winter coat

  • Waterproof boots with good grip
  • Umbrella (compact, windproof)
  • Lip balm and hand cream (dry air)
